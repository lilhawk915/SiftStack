## Bottom line update

**Probate:** Confidence is unchanged — fiduciary phone extraction is 100% precise; 73% recall is bounded by the scraper's case-selection logic, not the cap. **OK for daily cron** (today's cases are always in the recent window, so the selection bug doesn't bite). For multi-week historical backfills, the probate recall ceiling stays at ~75% until the scraper is patched.

**Foreclosure:** Confidence climbed substantially. **91% recall after the matcher cleanup**, with the remaining 2 misses on `2026 CV 0440` and `2026 CV 0517` — both with non-standard short case-number formats. Both DM and SS caught the SAME 21 cases across the 3 days when measured cleanly. The 4 "only-SS" cases on 6/01 (CV 03292-03295) look like real leads DM missed in her manual scrape.

**Recommendation:** **SiftStack is ready to replace the data manager's manual sheets** as the daily primary source for Montgomery County foreclosure + probate. Two follow-up fixes for the multi-week-backfill use case (not blocking daily cron):

1. **Investigate the probate scraper's year-query case-selection** — why aren't EST00895/EST00898 in the 345-case index?
2. **Handle short-form case numbers** like `2026 CV 0517` — normalize on the SS side so the bridge matches against DM's mixed-format entries.
